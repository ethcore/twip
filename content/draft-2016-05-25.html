###### 25-05-2016 # This week in Parity #2 Hello and welcome to second issue of *This week in Parity*! Parity is world's fastest and lightest Ethereum client. Written in the sophisticated, cutting-edge Rust language. This is a weekly summary of its progress and community. ## What's new in Parity? - [@gavofyork](https://github.com/gavofyork) fixed up the seal fields in RPC output [#1096](https://github.com/ethcore/parity/pull/1096) -
